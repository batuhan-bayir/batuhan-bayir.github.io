Batuhan Bayir

I am a second-year graduate student in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Utah. Before coming to Utah, I obtained my master's degree in mathematics from Ozyegin University and my bachelor's degree in physics from Ankara University. I am interested in partial differential equations and currently I study nonlinear Fokker-Planck type systems arising from grain growth modeling of the polycrystalline materials. For get more idea, please check out our recent work.

My advisors are Yekaterina Epshteyn and William M Feldman.
